## Site Content Overview

### Primary Content Sections

1. **Land (Military) Applications**
   - Focus: Ground vehicle power distribution, tactical communications, soldier-worn systems, C4ISR
   - Key products: DEFENSE-Link® data cables, SurpreFlex® AD961, AD1044ZH®, HTZR/HTNR military wire, MIS cables, FG2000®
   - Applications: Armored vehicles, weapons systems interconnect, power distribution, ruggedized data transmission

2. **Air (Aerospace) Applications**
   - Focus: Airframe, avionics, power distribution, high-speed data, drone tethers
   - Specifications: M81044, M22759, M27500, M16878, M1553, M5086, WC55021
   - Materials: Cross-linked polyalkene, ETFE, polyethylene, modified polyalkene
   - Product families: AD4, AD50, AD2®, wire wrap (PVDF/ETFE insulation)

3. **Sea (Shipboard) Applications**
   - Focus: Naval vessels, EMP protection, low-smoke zero-halogen solutions, water-blocked data
   - Specifications: M24640, M24643, M17, M85045
   - Signature products: Surprenant EMZ® (top-side EMP protection), Dry-Block technology
   - Sub-categories: Power distribution, thermocouple, multi-coaxial, flexing cables, communication with circuit integrity

4. **Space Applications**
   - Focus: Launch systems, satellite systems, radiation resistance, lightweight designs
   - Technology: Low-fluorine cables, fluorine-free solutions, red plague resistant
   - Product types: Unshielded/shielded Surprenant, space cables (Type 1A/1B, 2A/3A, 2B/3B)
   - Materials: Low-fluorine cross-linked ETFE

5. **Custom Cable Manufacturing**
   - Emphasizes engineering capabilities for specialty/custom solutions
   - Markets ability to handle demanding applications with talented engineering teams

6. **Technical Resources**
   - Materials reference data and compliance certifications
   - Polymer cross-linking information
   - Metric/temperature conversion data
   - Copper conductor data
   - Silver & nickel alloy conductor data
   - Glossary of technical terms
   - Page 67/Color code standards
   - Counterfeit parts policy

7. **Company Information**
   - About/overview presentation
   - Markets served
   - Manufacturing capabilities
   - Authorized distributors directory
   - Careers section
   - Contact information
   - Terms & conditions (purchase and sale)

8. **News & Updates**
   - Recent articles on NEMA WC 27500-2025 release
